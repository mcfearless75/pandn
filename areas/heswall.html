Electrical Services in Heswall

Heswall is one of Wirral's most desirable postcodes — characterised by large detached properties, generous gardens and long private driveways. It's an area where homeowners invest in their properties, and where the standard of electrical work needs to match.

P&N Electrical Services has carried out a growing number of jobs across Heswall — from garden and driveway lighting transformations to EICR testing on period properties. We understand the higher expectations that come with working in this part of the Wirral, and we deliver accordingly.

Outdoor & Garden Lighting in Heswall

The larger gardens and private drives common in Heswall make it one of the best areas on the Wirral for outdoor lighting installation. A professionally designed system can dramatically improve the security, safety and visual appeal of a property — illuminating pathways, highlighting garden features and making driveways safer and more welcoming after dark.

We install everything from simple porch and gate lighting to more ambitious multi-zone garden systems — all properly wired, weatherproof and designed to last. Every installation is finished cleanly with no surface damage left behind.

EICR Testing for Heswall Homeowners

Many of Heswall's older properties — particularly those built in the mid-twentieth century — have electrical installations that haven't been formally tested in years. An EICR gives you a complete picture of the condition of your wiring, consumer unit and earthing arrangements. We issue a clear, photo-illustrated report with straightforward recommendations.

Extensions and Outbuildings
