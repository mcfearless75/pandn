EICR Testing in Meols

Older bungalows and semis common in Meols sometimes have wiring that dates back to the 1960s or 1970s. If your home hasn't been tested recently, an EICR will give you a clear picture of what's there and what — if anything — needs attention. We provide clear, photo-backed reports with plain-English recommendations.
