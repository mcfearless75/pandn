Electrical Services in Wallasey

Wallasey is home to a wide mix of properties — from the Victorian and Edwardian terraces of Seacombe and Egremont to the bungalows and semis along the New Brighton seafront. Much of the housing stock dates back decades, which means ageing wiring, outdated fuse boards, and electrical systems that haven't seen a proper inspection in years.

P&N Electrical Services works across Wallasey every week, helping homeowners and landlords keep their properties safe, compliant, and up to date. Whether you need an EICR for a sale, a full rewire of an older property, or new outdoor lighting along a driveway or garden — we get the job done properly.

Why Wallasey Properties Often Need Attention

Many properties in Wallasey — particularly in the streets around Seacombe, Poulton and Liscard — were built before modern wiring standards. Older homes frequently have rubber-insulated cables, round-pin sockets, or outdated consumer units that no longer meet current regulations. If you're a landlord, an EICR is a legal requirement every five years. If you're buying or selling, your solicitor or mortgage lender will often ask for one.

We carry out EICR testing across all of Wallasey — producing a clear, photo-backed report with straightforward advice, not a list of scare tactics.
