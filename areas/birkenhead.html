Electrical Services in Birkenhead

As the largest town on the Wirral, Birkenhead has one of the most varied housing mixes on Merseyside — from large Victorian terraces and conversion flats in Oxton and Rock Ferry, to post-war semis in Prenton and modern new-builds closer to the waterfront. This variety means electrical needs vary enormously from property to property.

P&N Electrical Services works throughout Birkenhead regularly, carrying out everything from quick fault-finding jobs to full rewires of older terrace properties. Many of Birkenhead's older homes still have wiring that predates modern safety standards — a problem that often only surfaces when appliances start tripping the breaker, or when a buyer's survey flags an issue.

EICR Testing for Birkenhead Landlords

Birkenhead has a significant private rented sector — particularly in the streets around Hamilton Square, Conway Street and Tranmere. Since 2020, all rental properties in England require a valid EICR (Electrical Installation Condition Report) every five years, with a copy provided to tenants and the local council on request.

We carry out EICR testing across Birkenhead at fixed, transparent prices — starting from £95 for a one-bedroom property. No call-out fees. A clear report is issued, with photos and plain-English recommendations.

Consumer Unit Upgrades in Older Properties

A large proportion of Birkenhead properties, particularly those built before the 1980s, are still running on outdated consumer units (fuse boxes) that use rewirable fuses rather than modern circuit breakers and RCDs. These offer significantly less protection and don't meet current BS 7671 standards. We upgrade consumer units across Birkenhead quickly and with minimal disruption to your day.
